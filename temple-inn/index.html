Welcome to Temple Inn & Suites!

About Us

We are a hotel chain whose primary focus is providing housing for temple patrons. Our hotels are outfitted to meet all of the basic temple-goer neads. We provide housing, food, family history resources, and much more. Our goal is to help you have the best experience possible as you prepare to serve in the house of the Lord.

Coming Soon: Bountiful

We currently have 4 locations around the U.S., with our newest being the Arizona Phoenix location. On June 1st we officially anounced the construction of our fourth location: Bountiful, Utah.

About the Bountiful Temple

The Bountiful, Utah temple was anounced on February 2nd, 1990 and was dedicated in January of 1995. The temple was dedicated by President Howard W. Hunter and was the 47th temple dedicated in this dispensation.

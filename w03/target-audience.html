Target Audience

Persona #1

Fictional name:

  • Arthur Johnson

Job title/major responsibilities:

  • Small Bussiness Owner

Demographics:

  • 31 Years Old
  • Married
  • Father of 1 Child
  • Has a Degree in Bussiness

Goals and tasks:

  • Keep the company up and running
  • Grow the company

Environment:

  • Works in a hands-on environment with his employees. Spends much of his time overseeing the manufacturing of his product and the wellbeing of his employees.

Quote:

  • "What can we do to improve our reach?"

Persona #2

Fictional name:

  • Regina Lewis

Job title/major responsibilities:

  • Hotel Owner

Demographics:

  • 45 Years Old
  • Married
  • Mother of 4 Children
  • Has a Degree in Hotel and Restaurant Management

Goals and tasks:

  • Keep the hotel up and running
  • Improve the quality of the hotel

Spends her work time:

  • Ensuring employee and customer needs are met
  • Looking for and implimenting improvements to the hotel

Environment:

  • Generally works remotely
  • Makes an apperence every week or so to ensure the hotel is being run properly, and company standards are being met
  • Communicates frequently via email with others outside the company regarding events, hotel improvements, etc.
  • Holds in person and zoom meetings frequently with hotel management

Quote:

  • "How can we improve the quality of our customer service?"

Design Principles

For Visually Apealing Websites

Below are Several Examples of Effective Design Principles in Action:

Proximity: Rentler

Rentler's website is an excellent example of good Proximity. It creates an aesthetically pleasing design by keeping related elements together and keeping unrelated elements apart.

Alignment: Target

Target's website is a perfect example of alignment. Most of the images on this page fit along the same axis which gives it a very organized feel.

Contrast: Ancestry

Ancestry is a good example of contrast. On this page, for instance, we can see that there are light/white colors in the forground/text and dark colors in the background. This allows users to easily read the text. It also offers a clean proffesional look and feel to the site.
